RED BUCKSHOTS ARE LIVE, SO WHOEVER YOU SHOOT, YOURSELF OR THE DEALER WILL TAKE DAMAGE AND THE SHOOTHERS TURN WILL END

THE PLAYER AND DEALER TAKES TURNS SHOOTING, LAST ONE STANDING WINS
